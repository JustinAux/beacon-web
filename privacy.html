Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Effective: May 19, 2026

The short version: Beacon reads your bank data to show you your finances. We never sell your data, never move your money, and you can delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

Beacon Finance ("Beacon", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Beacon personal finance mobile application. Our contact email is privacy@beaconfinance.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data from connected services:

  • Account information — your name, email address, date of birth, and salary when you create a profile
  • Financial data — bank account balances, transaction history, and account metadata retrieved from Plaid after you connect your bank
  • Goals and debts — savings goals, debt balances, and payoff plans you enter manually
  • AI conversations — messages you send to the AI Advisor and the responses generated
  • Usage data — app open timestamps and device type, stored locally on your device only
  • Subscription data — your plan status and billing information processed by Stripe

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to provide Beacon's features:

  • Display your account balances, spending, and net worth
  • Categorize transactions and generate budget summaries
  • Power AI-generated financial advice personalized to your situation
  • Calculate retirement projections and debt payoff timelines
  • Send notifications you opt into (bill reminders, budget alerts)
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Beacon.

4. Bank Data & Plaid

Beacon connects to your financial institutions through Plaid Technologies, Inc., a regulated financial data provider. When you link your bank:

  • Your bank credentials are entered directly into Plaid's secure interface — Beacon never sees or stores your username or password
  • We request read-only access only — Beacon cannot initiate transfers, make payments, or modify your accounts in any way
  • Plaid is SOC 2 Type II certified and uses bank-level encryption
  • You can revoke Plaid access at any time from within the app or at my.plaid.com

Plaid's privacy policy is available at plaid.com/legal.

5. AI Advisor

When you use the AI Advisor, a summary of your financial context (spending patterns, debt balances, goals, and salary) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalized responses.

  • Anthropic does not train its models on API traffic by default
  • Your conversations are stored encrypted in your Beacon account
  • Only you can view your conversation history
  • You can delete all conversations at any time

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database:

  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • All data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3
  • Your data is logically isolated — no other Beacon user can access it
  • Row-level security policies ensure database queries only return your own data

7. Biometric Authentication

If you enable Face ID or fingerprint lock, your biometric data never leaves your device. Beacon only receives a success or failure signal from your device's operating system. We have no access to your biometric data at any time.

8. Payments & Stripe

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. Beacon never stores your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details. All payment information is handled directly by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. Stripe's privacy policy is at stripe.com/privacy.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or financial data with third parties, except:

  • Plaid — to retrieve your bank data (with your explicit authorization)
  • Anthropic — to generate AI advisor responses (financial context only, not your identity)
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments
  • Supabase — for secure data storage and authentication
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our legal rights

None of these providers are permitted to use your data for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

10. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — update inaccurate information in your profile at any time
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect — revoke Plaid bank access without deleting your account
  • Export — request an export of your data by contacting us

To delete your account: open Beacon → Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers, revokes Plaid access, and cancels your subscription. Deletion is irreversible.

To make any other data request, email privacy@beaconfinance.app. We will respond within 48 hours.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 24 hours. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts beyond 30 days.

12. Children's Privacy

Beacon is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notice at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Beacon after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

For any privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@beaconfinance.app
  • Support: support@beaconfinance.app
